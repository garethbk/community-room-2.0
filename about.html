We hope to create spaces for multimedia artists, musicians, performers, presenters, and cross disciplinary makers to share their work.

Our goal is to host reoccurring events at different spots around the city. We are currently accepting submissions for our second event, check the "submit" page for more details.

Grace Huddleston

Southern born, VCUarts educated. Grace works primarily in performance, textiles and drawing. Her source of performance inspiration comes from Joan Jonas, Thu Tran, YACHT, Miranda July, and Lygia Clark.

She and Rachel met in 2010 and have been collaborators since 2013 when they put on their first joint show "Safety Dance" in Grace's college house.

Though performance is a relatively new medium for Grace, she just feels right when she communicates her ideas in that way and she is excited about the promise of Richmond's live art community.
